About Suki

Built by students,
for students.

Suki was born from a simple observation: students have stuff they don't need, and other students need stuff they can't afford new. We created a safe, campus-focused marketplace that eliminates the risks of traditional online trading.

Every user is verified with their university email, every transaction happens on campus, and our community ratings ensure you're always dealing with trusted peers.

How It Works

Start trading in
three simple steps.

01

Sign Up with Your School Email

Download the app and verify your student status with your university email. It takes less than a minute, and your account is instantly linked to your campus community.

02

Browse or List Items

Looking to buy? Browse categories from textbooks to furniture. Selling? Snap photos, set your price, and publish. Your listing is visible only to students on your campus.

03

Meet & Exchange Safely

Use our in-app chat to arrange a meeting at one of your campus's designated safe exchange spots. Complete the trade and leave a rating to build trust.
